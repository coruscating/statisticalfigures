 Farewell
On behalf of the Japan Skating Federation it is with great pleasure to thank all
skaters, who showed a very high-level competition, the judges and officials for
their work, the International Skating Union for their trust in our organization
as well as all those who were involved to make this ISU Grand Prix of Figure
Skating Final such a great success.
As a former Olympian myself, I truly understand the skaters focus on the
upcoming Olympic Winter Games 2018 in PyeongChang, Korea. Therefore, I
wish all skaters who competed here at this Final that their Olympic dream may
become reality.
We wish everyone a safe trip back home and a successful remaining skating
season. The Japan Skating Federation is very much looking forward to
welcome you again in Japan in the very near future.
Best regards,
 Seiko Hashimoto
